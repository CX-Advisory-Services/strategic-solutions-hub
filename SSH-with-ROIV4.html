Commercial Models

We offer flexible commercial models to suit different client needs and risk appetites. Explore the options to find the best fit for your innovation goals.

Tiered Access & Subscription Model (with "Freemium" Option)

  • loyalty Limited "Taste" (Freemium): Access to thought leadership, general workshops, high-level assessments, and limited knowledge base. Ideal for lead generation and building relationships.
  • credit_card Standard Tier: Monthly/annual subscription for defined "innovation credits" or hours and participation in smaller incubation projects. Provides ongoing value and recurring revenue.
  • stars Premium Tier: Higher subscription unlocks unlimited/larger "innovation credits," priority access to our team, larger/more complex projects, and dedicated resources. Perfect for significant innovation needs.
  • request_quote Project-Based Fees: Transparent additional fees for projects exceeding allocated credits/hours across all tiers (above Basic). Ensures flexibility and fair compensation for extensive engagements.

Gain-Sharing Model (Performance-Based)

Agree on specific, measurable KPIs for Strategic Solutions Hub projects (e.g., cost reduction, efficiency gains, revenue increase). A percentage of the achieved improvement is shared with our organization.

  • check_circle Pros: Strongly aligns incentives, direct reward for tangible results, clear path to ROI, potentially very profitable.
  • error_outline Cons: Requires careful KPI negotiation, high trust/transparency, revenue dependent on client's implementation success, complex measurement attribution.

Project Process Flow:

1. Engagement & KPI Definition: Identify opportunity and agree on measurable KPIs that the Strategic Solutions Hub projects will impact, and the gain-sharing percentage.

2. Contracting: Formalize KPIs, measurement methodology, and terms in a legal agreement.

3. Project Execution: The Strategic Solutions Hub implements the solution to impact the defined KPIs.

4. Performance Measurement: Post-implementation, monitor KPIs and calculate the value of the gains.

5. Verification & Reward: Present gains to the client for verification, then invoice for the shared percentage.

Co-Investment Model (Shared Risk, Shared Reward)

We will both invest in the Strategic Solutions Hub's infrastructure and project costs (e.g., 50/50 split). Both parties share in the benefits (IP ownership, cost savings, revenue generated).

  • thumb_up Pros: Demonstrates commitment, reduces your upfront financial burden, fosters deeper partnership.
  • warning_amber Cons: Requires high trust/collaboration, complex legal/operational structure, shared risk if projects are unsuccessful.

Zenith Loyalty Program: "Innovation Credits" Model

Take part in our Zenith Loyalty Program and let's establish your "innovation credits". The Strategic Solutions Hub proposes projects, and you approve them based on your strategic priorities and potential ROI.

  • star_half Pros: Enjoy innovation credits that you earn by being a member.
  • sentiment_satisfied_alt Cons: None.

Outcome-Based Pricing (Fixed Fee for Defined Outcomes)

Let's agree on a fixed price for achieving a pre-defined, measurable outcome, rather than hourly rates or project-based fees. Example: "Reduce customer service call handling time by 15% for a fixed fee of $X."

  • verified_user Pros: Provides price certainty, focuses on delivering value.
  • block Cons: Requires precise understanding of problem/solution.

Your Return on Investment: A Partnership for Measurable Growth

Engaging with our Strategic Solutions Hub is not a cost; it is a strategic investment in the future resilience and efficiency of your operations. Our "innovation playground" is designed as a powerful engine to deliver a multifaceted return, directly impacting your bottom line, customer loyalty, and long-term technological health. We measure our success by your success, focusing on three core pillars of ROI:

1. Drive Significant Cost Optimization

The Strategic Solutions Hub provides a controlled environment to re-engineer processes and test technologies before you commit to a large-scale rollout. This "test-before-you-invest" model systematically de-risks innovation and is a direct path to significant cost savings.

  • tune Process Improvement Initiatives: By mapping your current workflows, we can pilot automation (RPA), streamline complex tasks, and eliminate redundant activities, leading to direct cost reduction.
  • rocket_launch Efficient Technology Deployment: We ensure you deploy the right technology, configured the right way, for the right problem, preventing costly missteps and ensuring effective value from day one.

2. Elevate Key Customer Experience (CX) Metrics

Superior customer experience is a direct result of efficient, effective, and empathetic internal processes. The Strategic Solutions Hub focuses on initiatives that not only cut costs but also create smoother, faster, and more satisfying journeys for your customers.

  • sentiment_satisfied Customer Satisfaction (CSAT) and Net Promoter Score (NPS): By resolving issues quickly and leveraging AI for personalized interactions, we create positive experiences that boost CSAT scores and turn detractors into loyal promoters.
  • support_agent First Contact Resolution (FCR): We test and deploy new knowledge management systems, agent-assist AI tools, and optimized workflows to empower frontline teams to solve customer issues on the very first try, improving satisfaction and lowering costs.
  • timer Average Handling Time (AHT) and Service Levels: We identify and resolve system or process bottlenecks, reducing AHT without sacrificing quality, enabling you to meet and exceed service level agreements consistently.

3. Lower the Total Cost of Ownership (TCO) for Technology

The initial purchase price of technology is only a fraction of its true cost. Our Strategic Solutions Hub focuses on ensuring your technology deployments are successful and sustainable, which dramatically lowers your TCO over its entire lifecycle.

  • inventory_2 Avoiding "Shelf-ware": By validating technology against your real-world use cases, we ensure high user adoption and prevent investment in powerful tools that end up unused.
  • link Optimized for Integration and Scalability: We prototype solutions to ensure they integrate seamlessly with your existing technology stack and are architected to scale effectively, preventing massive, unforeseen costs.
  • build Reduced Long-Term Support Costs: A well-designed and properly deployed system is inherently more stable, requiring less ongoing maintenance, fewer support tickets, and less administrative overhead, contributing directly to a lower TCO.
